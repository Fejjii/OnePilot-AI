# NovaEdge Solutions — Refund Policy

This policy applies to all paid NovaEdge engagements: Pilot, Standard Implementation,
Enterprise Implementation, and Managed Automation Retainers. It is referenced by the master
services agreement (MSA) and overrides any conflicting verbal commitment.

## Guiding Principles
- Customers should never feel trapped.
- We refund proactively when we have not earned the fee.
- We do not negotiate refund amounts in tickets. Decisions follow this policy.

## Pilot Refunds
A 4-week Pilot is **fully refundable within the first 7 days** if the customer chooses not to
proceed. After day 7:

- We refund **75%** of the Pilot fee if the customer cancels before day 14.
- We refund **50%** if cancelled before day 21.
- After day 21 the Pilot is non-refundable. Most of the work has been done by then.

## Standard Implementation Refunds
Standard Implementations are billed 50% upfront, 25% at midpoint, 25% on go-live.

- Upfront 50% is non-refundable once kickoff has occurred. Kickoff is defined as the
  scheduled call where we receive customer credentials.
- Midpoint payment is non-refundable.
- Go-live payment is contingent on the agreed success criteria from the SOW. If the success
  criteria are missed, we either:
  - Continue work at no extra charge for up to 4 weeks, **or**
  - Refund the go-live payment in full at the customer's choice.

## Enterprise Implementation Refunds
Refund terms for Enterprise engagements are negotiated in the SOW. Default is:

- 25% kickoff payment non-refundable after kickoff.
- Subsequent milestones refundable pro-rata if NovaEdge cancels.
- Customer-initiated cancellation forfeits the next milestone payment.

## Retainer Refunds
Managed Automation Retainers are billed monthly in advance.

- Customers can cancel at any time. Cancellation stops the **next** billing cycle; the
  current month is non-refundable.
- Annual prepay customers who cancel mid-term receive a refund of **75%** of the remaining
  months on the contract.

## Service Credits (Instead of Refunds)
If we miss an SLA (see `escalation_policy.md`), the customer receives **service credits**:

- 5% of the monthly retainer for each missed P1 response SLA.
- 3% of the monthly retainer for each missed P2 response SLA.
- Maximum service credit per month: **25%** of the monthly retainer.

Service credits are applied to the next invoice. We do not pay credits in cash.

## What Is Not Refundable
- Third-party LLM token costs that have already been incurred.
- Per-conversation overages billed during the customer's active period.
- Compliance add-ons that have already been delivered.
- Custom integrations once the integration has been delivered and accepted.

## How to Request a Refund
Email `billing@novaedge.io` from your account-admin email with:

- Workspace ID (`org_*`).
- Engagement name and SOW reference.
- Reason for the request.
- Preferred remedy (refund or credit).

We respond within **3 business days** with a decision.

## Disputes
If a customer disagrees with a refund decision, the escalation path is:

1. Head of Customer Success.
2. CFO (`finance-lead@novaedge.io`).
3. CEO.

We will provide a final written decision within 10 business days of the initial dispute.
After that, the customer's remedies are as defined in the MSA.

## Edge Cases
- **Force majeure:** if a regulatory event makes it illegal to deliver the service in the
  customer's region, we refund the unused balance in full.
- **Acquisition / change of control:** the surviving entity inherits the contract. Refunds
  follow normal rules.
- **Trial overruns:** if you forget to cancel a free Pilot extension and we begin a paid
  retainer, the first **15 days** of that retainer are refundable on request.
