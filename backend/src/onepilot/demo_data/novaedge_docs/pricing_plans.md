# NovaEdge Solutions — Pricing Plans

All prices in **USD**, exclusive of taxes. Billed monthly unless noted otherwise.
Annual contracts receive a **10% discount** on retainer fees.

## Project Engagements

| Engagement                  | Duration  | Price            | Best for                                                                 |
|----------------------------|-----------|------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Discovery Call             | 60 min    | **Free**         | First conversation, scoping, qualification.                              |
| Pilot                       | 4 weeks   | **$4,900**       | One workflow, one team, proof of value.                                  |
| Standard Implementation     | 8 weeks   | **$14,900**      | Two or three workflows, full integration, go-live.                       |
| Enterprise Implementation   | 12+ weeks | **Custom**       | Multi-team rollouts, compliance reviews, custom integrations.            |

Project engagements include integration setup, knowledge-base ingestion, prompt tuning,
documentation, and a 30-day post-launch tuning window.

## Managed Automation Retainers
After implementation, most customers move to a monthly retainer for ongoing optimization,
monitoring, and incident response.

### Essentials — $1,800 / month
- Up to **2,000 AI-handled conversations** per month.
- **3 hours** of expert support per month.
- Standard SLA: response within 1 business day.
- Monthly performance report.
- Best for: small teams, one workflow live.

### Growth — $3,800 / month
- Up to **10,000 AI-handled conversations** per month.
- **10 hours** of expert support per month.
- SLA: response within 4 business hours.
- Quarterly business review with a CSM.
- Includes 1 prompt/workflow refresh per quarter.
- Best for: scaling support, multiple workflows.

### Scale — $7,500 / month
- Up to **40,000 AI-handled conversations** per month.
- **25 hours** of expert support per month.
- SLA: response within 1 business hour, 24/5.
- Monthly business review with a dedicated CSM.
- 2 prompt/workflow refreshes per quarter.
- Best for: mid-market companies, multi-region usage.

### Enterprise — Custom
- Custom volume, custom SLAs (24/7 available).
- Dedicated Slack channel with the NovaEdge team.
- Named CSM and named technical lead.
- Optional on-prem or VPC deployment of the inference stack.
- Custom data residency and security review.

## Add-ons
- **Additional workflow:** $2,500 one-time per workflow after Standard Implementation.
- **Custom integration:** starts at $3,500 (scoped per project).
- **Compliance package** (SOC 2 questionnaire support, DPA, custom DPIA): $4,000 / year.
- **Premium model usage** (GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, large context): metered separately,
  pass-through cost + 15% margin.

## Volume Overages
If a retainer exceeds its included conversation limit:

- Essentials: $0.18 per additional conversation.
- Growth: $0.12 per additional conversation.
- Scale: $0.08 per additional conversation.

Overages are billed at the end of the month. A soft warning email is sent at 80% of plan limit
and a hard warning at 100%.

## Discounts
- **Annual prepay:** 10% off retainer.
- **Two-year commitment:** 15% off retainer (no further negotiation expected).
- **Non-profit:** 20% off retainer with proof of status.
- **Early-stage startup** (< 50 employees, < $5M raised): 25% off Pilot only.

## What Pricing Does Not Include
- LLM token costs above the included allowance (passed through with markup).
- Customer-side calendar, CRM, helpdesk, or storage licensing.
- Custom legal review (we use a standard MSA + DPA — both available on request).

## How We Quote
For non-standard requests, we quote within **2 business days** of the discovery call.
All quotes are valid for **30 days**. We do not engage in last-mile bidding wars; pricing
above the published rate card requires VP-level sign-off (see `objection_handling.md` for
the discount rules our reps must follow).
