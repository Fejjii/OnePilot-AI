# 30-Second Pitch

OnePilot AI is an AI operations workspace for small businesses — not a thin chatbot wrapper.

It answers questions from a company’s own knowledge base with citations, drafts emails, proposes meetings, and tracks leads. Anything that would touch the outside world — Gmail, Calendar, CRM — stops for human approval first.

The public demo is live: open the site, click **Try the demo**, and you’re in a seeded workspace. Gmail and Calendar are simulated so reviewers can safely walk the approval path end to end.

Under the hood it’s FastAPI, LangGraph, PostgreSQL, Redis, Qdrant, and Next.js — with multi-tenant isolation, audit logs, and provider adapters that run mock or live without rewriting the product.
